Word Guesser Game

Choose your mode and test your guessing skills!

Singleplayer 2 Players

Game Modes

Choose your preferred game mode and test your word-guessing skills!

Singleplayer Mode: Start with an easy word and progress to more challenging levels. As the levels increase, words become longer, you get fewer hearts, and time gets tighter. Words and their descriptions are automatically generated by AI to keep the experience fresh and exciting!
Two-Player Mode: Play with a friend in a competitive turn-based format. Select a difficulty level, which determines word length, number of hearts, and time limits—just like in Singleplayer. Think of a word for your opponent, and AI will generate a description for it. Take turns to see who is the ultimate word guesser!

Game Powers

Use special powers strategically to gain an advantage!

Hide Letters: Hides the opponent's word for one move, making it harder for them to guess the word.
Remove Letter: Removes one of the opponent's guessed letters. The letter could be correct or incorrect, disrupting their progress.
Reveal Letter: Reveals one random letter from your word. It might be a letter you’ve already guessed, so think carefully before using it!
Free Guess: Allows you to guess a letter without consequences. If your guess is wrong, you won't lose a heart, but if it's correct, the power will be wasted.
